Game Rules:
Simple FOMO is a game theory based lottery that now rewards both the last person to enter and the person with the most entries with the entire pot split evenly between them.
  • The initial pot size is 2 ETH.
  • The initial deadline is Nov 16th, 2019 14:10:54 UTC. Check lottery details to view the current deadline.
  • The deadline can be extended by submitting an entry.
  • Multiple entries from the same address are allowed.
  • The initial extension time is 10 minutes. Each entry increases the extension time by 0.5 seconds.
  • The deadline will only be extended if at the time of the last entry, the extension time is beyond the deadline.
  • After the deadline, no more entries will be accepted and the last person or the top entrant can claim the pot after 5 minutes.
  • The pot will be split evenly between the top entrant and the last entrant when it is claimed.
  • The initial cost to enter is 0.001 ETH. For every 10 entries, the cost increases by 0.005 ETH.
  • Usage fee is 2.5% of entry cost, deducted at time of entry from the cost.
